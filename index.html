Projects

Some of the ongoing 'Human Behavior Sensing' projects are presented here in brief. Click here to access my complete list of patents and publications

  1. Classification and modelling of Oxygen Saturation in blood (SpO2) and cough detection (during sleep) solely using PPG signal, from Empatica E4 smartwatch for early stage COVID-19 detection. Analysis and testing of trained model has also been done on MIMIC II dataset.
  2. Early stage detection of COVID-19 from PPG signal collected using E4 watch
  3. Creativity and Cognitive Load Assessment of an individual based on his/her PPG signal (in-house data collected, alongwith WESAD dataset) while he/she is subjected to some stimuli tests for the same.
  4. Role fitment of an employee in a particular job based on his/her natural propensity in handling technical/creative/innovative job through analysis of physiological signals
  5. Affective detection of mental health of an employee in a job (enterprise scenario) based on their respiration signal. Initial model built on Affective Pacman dataset
  6. Securing IoT devices, by authenticating an user during a session, using PPG and Accelerometer data, collected from smart wearable devices.
